# Block Prompt Templates
# 에이전트가 각 블록 이미지 생성 시 extra_context로 주입하는 힌트

[히어로]
This is the HERO section - the first thing customers see.
Include: product hero image, main headline, sub-headline.
Make it appetite-inducing with premium food photography style (sizzle shot).
DO NOT include price unless explicitly in the copy text.

[셀링포인트]
This is the KEY SELLING POINTS section.
Include: 3-5 selling points with icons and short descriptions.
Layout: grid or card-based layout, each point clearly separated.
Icons should be simple and modern.

[시즐컷]
This is the FOOD VISUAL GALLERY section.
Include: multiple appetizing food photos showing different angles.
Focus on texture, steam, freshness, cross-sections.
Layout: collage or grid of food images.

[소셜프루프]
This is the SOCIAL PROOF section.
Include: review highlights, star ratings, purchase count, repurchase rate.
Layout: review cards with photos, statistics in large numbers.

[원재료]
This is the INGREDIENTS/ORIGIN STORY section.
Include: origin location, farm/producer photos, ingredient close-ups.
Show the journey from farm to table.

[제조공정]
This is the MANUFACTURING PROCESS section.
Include: production facility, HACCP/certification badges, hygiene standards.
Step-by-step visual flow of production process.

[맛설명]
This is the TASTE/TEXTURE DESCRIPTION section.
Include: taste profile visualization, texture descriptions.
Use sensory language visualized: sweetness scale, flavor wheel, etc.

[조리법]
This is the RECIPE/COOKING GUIDE section.
Include: step-by-step cooking instructions with photos.
Show microwave/air-fryer/stove options with time/temperature.

[영양정보]
This is the NUTRITION HIGHLIGHT section.
Include: key nutrition facts in marketing-friendly format.
Visualize: protein/calories/etc as infographic, compare to daily recommended.

[보관해동]
This is the STORAGE/THAWING GUIDE section.
Include: storage temperature, duration, thawing methods.
Visual icons for freezer/fridge/room temperature.

[브랜드스토리]
This is the BRAND/PRODUCER STORY section.
Include: founder/producer photo, brand philosophy, founding story.
Warm, personal, trust-building tone.

[FAQ]
This is the FAQ section.
Include: 4-6 common questions and answers.
Clean, accordion-style or card layout.

[가격구성]
This is the PRICING/BUNDLE section.
Include: single vs set options, per-unit price comparison.
Highlight "most popular" option with badge.

[배송]
This is the SHIPPING INFO section.
Include: cold-chain/ice-pack packaging, delivery timeline.
Icons for refrigerated/frozen/room-temp shipping.

[CTA]
This is the CALL-TO-ACTION section.
Include: urgent/limited offer messaging, large purchase button.
Create urgency: limited stock, time-limited discount, first-purchase coupon.

[법적표기]
This is the LEGAL/COMPLIANCE section.
Include: nutrition facts table, allergens, origin, expiration info.
Clean, table-based layout matching platform standards.
